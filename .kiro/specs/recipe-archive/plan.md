# Mom's Recipes

## Requirements
- Digital copies of family recipies.
    - Scannable / can upload a photo of a recipe
    - Image to text transcription (easyOCR?)
    - Editable to fix mistakes
- Printable on 4x6 cue cards
    - additional sizes?
    - dual-sided printing
- Attach and sort/filter by metadata (e.g. "cookies", "breakfast", "pastry")
- WYSIWYG web editor for layout?
- Layout templates for each size
    - e.g. sections for author, title, ingredients and steps.
    - Can create and manage new templates
- Support for images
